What is it?

Top

lov3ly.me is an online beauty contest. You can upload personal photos and find out how attractive or photogenic you are.

You can compete or vote on photos in the general section which can be filtered using the Filter Bar, or you photos in Contests and compete there.

lov3ly.me is not all about competing against others. You can upload as many photos as you like and find out which one is the most appreciated. You can change your hair style, clothes and make-up to see which style suites you best.

Uploading Photos

Top

You can upload photos using the My photos menu item from the top of the page.

You are presented with an upload form where you can select the photo by clicking the Choose File button.

Finding a catchy title and description can add to your photo appeal.

If you un-check the Active option than your photo will be hidden for the rest of the users.

If your photo represents you at a younger age or was taken while you were living somewhere else, you can change all these expanding Extra info section

After selecting the file you are presented with a cropping dialog where you can select only a part of your image. This part is mandatory if the photo contains other people or animals.

Contests

Top

You can create contest by selecting My Contest from the top menu bar.

Entering a descriptive title and complete description will reduce the number of unwanted users that apply for your contest.

When creating a contest you can enter Country and/or a City to make your contest specific to a geographical region. These are used for the users that use the Filter Bar to browse the site.

If you do not check the Verify before accepting? option the users will be abble to register photos in your contest without your approval.

Admission Deadline is the deadline date for users to enroll photos in your contes. After the admission is over the voting is enabled and lasts until the Voting Deadline. Both periods can be maximum one month but is advisable to make them shorter so the users will not loose thei patience. Find the best ranges for your context.
You can see the status of your contests or approve user enrollment using the contest table in this section.

Comments

Top

If you are logged in you can post comments for photos and contests. Pressing the send button or the enter key will send the message

Tip You can jump to a new line in your message by holding the Shift key and pressing Enter key.

Private messages

Top

Private message is sent to a specific user and only that user can read the message.

To read your messages you can click on the My Messages on the top menu bar.

Filter Bar

Top

You can filter the photos and contests you see on the site using the filter bar located right below the top menu bar.

The filter is saved in your session so it will remain active until you change it ore clear it.

Tip You can clear all filter fields by pressing the x on the left of Filter button
