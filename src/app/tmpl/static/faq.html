FAQ

What can I upload on lov3ly.me?
You can upload only jpeg encoded photos representing your person. The photos may not contain images of other persons or animals, nudity, or other offending, discriminatory or disturbing images.
Can I upload photos of myself at a younger age?
Yes, when you do so make sure specify in your photo uploading form the age you had in that picture. Doing so prevents receiving fake reports on your photo.
What is the fake button used for?
You should use the fake button when you are sure that the photo does not represent the photo owner. Examples: the owner is male and the picture represents a female, you recognize the person in the photo or the owner and they do not match, the age of the owner cannot be the age of the person in the picture.
What is the abuse button used for?
You should use the abuse button when you see that the photo represents images of multiple persons, animals, nudity, or other offending, discriminatory or disturbing images.
When I delete a photo, is it really deleted?
Yes, when you delete a photo we delete the photo, the votes and the comments related to it. Exception are the photos that you enrolled in contests which are preserved in those contexts only.
How is the ranking score computed?
The ranking score is computed using the lower bound of Wilson score confidence interval. For more information you can read this article.
